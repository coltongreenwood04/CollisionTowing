## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Inconsistent NAP** (Name, Address, Phone)
   - Use EXACT same format everywhere
   - Don't abbreviate (West Jordan vs W Jordan)

2. **Ignoring Reviews**
   - Not asking for reviews = slow growth
   - Not responding to reviews = Google penalizes you

3. **Keyword Stuffing**
   - Don't overuse "towing" on every page
   - Write naturally for humans, not robots

4. **Duplicate Content**
   - Don't copy text from competitors
   - Google penalizes identical content

5. **Mobile Issues**
   - Your site is mobile-first (already optimized)
   - But always test after changes
